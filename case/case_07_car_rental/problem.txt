A car rental company has three types of cars: economy, midsize, and luxury. The company can rent out a maximum of 20 cars at any time. The company earns a profit of $30, $50, and $80 per day for each economy, midsize, and luxury car rented out, respectively. The company must rent out at least 2 luxury cars and the total number of midsize cars rented out must not exceed the number of economy cars rented out. How many cars of each type should the company rent out to maximize its profit?
